Food Delivery System

System allows user make an order via using mobile app or website of service, allowing him to look for local restaurants that are near his location or choose any different on his choice.

In this system there are three types of persons who take place in it:
1. Customer -
   Customer able to look through catalogs or list of restaurants, their assortment/menu, place their order in cart and form it with payment.
2. Vendor -
   Vendor as owner of restaurant may upload menu on their page, acquiring orders from their customers,know about it details and content, providing changes in status of an order.
3. Administrator of system -
   Having the wide specter of actions, revising list of establishments that are in system, add another one, ability to have full list of users from previous two categories, add a new user and block user if he broke rules of the platform
